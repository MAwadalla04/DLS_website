About the Symposium

This full-day symposium is designed to convene legal, academic, and policy experts to explore legal frameworks and ethical considerations in emergency and disaster response. The event will be open to city, state, and federal attorneys and legal professionals from partner agencies involved in emergency management, mitigation, and public safety.
